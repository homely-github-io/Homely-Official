Vision

In a world where people try to create the best life possible for themselves in foreign countries, we believe that only by thinking of good memories, obstacles can be overcome.

Bringing home to you

Connecting individuals to their heritage is immensely important to our brand as this is what we stand for and which has an emotional tie with their country of origin. Creating this connection benefits individuals and society as a whole. It translates to stimulating a sense of belonging, pride, and can lead to greater appreciation and respect for diverse cultures. Homely aims for expatriates maintaining their cultural identity and making them feel safe and comfortable being anywhere else but home. We seek to bring home to you.

Be Home-Healthy

It is something almost everyone feels every now and then, homesickness. Whether it is because you went on a 5-day school trip, or maybe you moved to a different city. You are separated from your friends and family. Some might deal with this better than others, but almost all feel homesick to a certain extent. Homely was created with the goal in mind to help you be home-healthy.
